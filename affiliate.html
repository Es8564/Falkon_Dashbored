Referral FAQ

How do I get my referral link?
After registering, your referral code appears in your Account page. Your link format is: falconai.trade/register.html?ref=YOUR_CODE
When do I get paid?
Commissions are calculated at the end of each month and paid within 7 business days via your chosen payment method.
Do I need to be a subscriber myself?
Yes. You need an active Falcon AI account (trial or paid) to participate in the referral program.
What if my referral cancels?
You earn commission only for months where your referral has an active subscription. If they cancel, commission stops. If they resubscribe, it resumes.
Is there a minimum payout?
Yes, the minimum payout threshold is £40. Commissions below this roll over to the next month.
Can I promote on social media?
Absolutely. Share on Twitter, Telegram, YouTube, trading forums, or anywhere else. Just don't make misleading profit claims — keep it honest.
