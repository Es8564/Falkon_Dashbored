Frequently Asked Questions — Everything You Need to Know

🚀 Getting Started
How do I connect my MT5 account?▼
Visit the onboarding page, enter your MT5 broker server name, login number, and investor password. Select your risk level, fund your wallet (minimum £1,000), and submit. Your account will be activated within 24 hours after admin verification.
Which brokers are supported?▼
We support all major MT5 brokers including IC Markets, Exness, Pepperstone, XM, FXTM, and most other MetaTrader 5 brokers. If your broker isn't listed in the dropdown, select "Other" and enter your server name manually.
What is the minimum investment?▼
The minimum wallet deposit is £1,000 USDT. FalconAI is designed for serious capital management. Accounts below this threshold cannot sustain proper position sizing and risk management.
Is my password safe?▼
Absolutely. Your MT5 credentials are encrypted with AES-256-GCM (the same encryption used by banks and military systems) before being stored. Your password is never visible in plaintext — not even to our administrators. The encryption key is stored in a hardware-secured environment variable on our dedicated London VPS.
💰 Billing & Performance Fees
How does the 30% performance fee work?▼
We use the High-Water Mark (HWM) model:

• We ONLY charge 30% of net new profits above your account's all-time high.
• If your account drops (drawdown), zero fees are charged until you fully recover AND make new profit.
• Example: Account goes $10,000 → $10,500 (new high). Fee = $500 × 30% = $150.
• If it drops to $9,800, NO fee until it exceeds $10,500 again.

This model ensures we only earn when YOU earn.
When are fees charged?▼
Fees are calculated and deducted daily at 00:05 UTC. The system checks your account balance, compares it to your High-Water Mark, and if there's new profit, 30% is deducted from your USDT wallet. You'll receive an email notification with a detailed invoice for every fee charge.
What happens if my wallet runs out?▼
⚠️ Important: If your wallet drops below £10 USDT after a fee deduction, you receive a 24-hour alert to top up. If the wallet is not funded within 24 hours, copy trading is automatically paused — no new trades are copied. Your existing positions remain open for manual management. To resume, simply top up your wallet.
How do I deposit to my wallet?▼
USDT deposits are accepted via TRC-20 (Tron) or ERC-20 (Ethereum). Contact support for your unique deposit address. Manual bank transfers are also accepted — reach out via the live chat for details.
📊 Trading & Risk Management
What instruments does FalconAI trade?▼
Our master system primarily trades Gold (XAUUSD) and Dow Jones (US30/DJ30), with selective exposure to US100 (Nasdaq) and Bitcoin. The EA uses a neural network with 5 parallel trading models for diversified signal generation.
What is the Risk Multiplier?▼
The risk multiplier controls your position size relative to the master:

• 0.5× — Conservative (half the master's lot size)
• 1.0× — Standard (proportional to your balance)
• 1.5× — Moderate aggressive
• 2.0× — Aggressive (double the proportional size)
• 3.0× — High risk (triple)

Higher multipliers mean higher potential returns AND higher risk. Choose based on your risk tolerance.
Can I change my risk level later?▼
Yes. Contact support to adjust your risk multiplier at any time. Changes take effect within 5 minutes on the next trade signal.
What is the expected performance?▼
Past performance does not guarantee future results. Our system targets consistent risk-adjusted returns using institutional-grade algorithms. You can view live performance metrics on the dashboard. Individual results vary based on risk multiplier, broker execution, and market conditions.
⚙️ Account Management
Can I disconnect at any time?▼
Yes. Use the Emergency Disconnect button in your dashboard, or contact support. Copy trading stops immediately. Your existing open positions remain under your control — you'll need to manage or close them manually through your MT5 terminal.
Do I keep control of my MT5 account?▼
Yes. FalconAI only copies trades TO your account. You retain full access and can trade manually alongside the copy system (though we recommend not interfering with copied positions to avoid conflicts).
What if I withdraw money from my MT5 account?▼
Withdrawals are automatically detected by our system. Your High-Water Mark is adjusted proportionally — ensuring you're never charged fees on capital you withdrew. For example, if you withdraw 30% of your balance, your HWM is reduced by 30%.
