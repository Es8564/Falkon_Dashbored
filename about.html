About Falcon AI

Building the future of intelligent automated trading.

Our Mission

Falcon AI was created with a single goal: to give every trader access to institutional-grade AI trading technology. We believe that intelligent automation, combined with proper risk management, can transform how people trade the financial markets.

Our platform combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with a user-friendly remote control system, allowing traders to manage their accounts from anywhere in the world — from their phone, tablet, or computer.

What Makes Us Different

Unlike traditional Expert Advisors that follow rigid rules, Falcon AI learns and adapts. Our self-improving algorithm analyzes market conditions in real-time, adjusts its strategy, and protects your capital through multiple layers of risk management.

We don't just sell software — we provide a complete trading ecosystem with live support, daily profit targets, remote control, and continuous updates.

Technology

Falcon AI is built on a modern, scalable architecture:

• Trading Engine: MQL5 Expert Advisor running on MetaTrader 5
• AI Layer: Self-learning algorithm with adaptive decision-making
• Backend: Google Apps Script for secure, serverless data management
• Dashboard: Progressive Web App (PWA) — installable on any device
• Security: HMAC-SHA256 signed communications, per-token isolation
