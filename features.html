Features

A complete trading ecosystem — not just an EA.

🧠 AI-Powered Trading Engine

Falcon AI uses a self-learning algorithm that continuously adapts to changing market conditions. Unlike static EAs with fixed rules, our AI analyzes patterns, learns from outcomes, and improves its decision-making over time.

  • Adaptive strategy that evolves with the market
  • Pattern recognition across multiple timeframes
  • Self-healing: detects and corrects its own mistakes
  • Cognitive state monitoring (learning, adapting, innovating)
  • Health score tracking for AI performance

📱 Remote Control Dashboard

Control your EA from anywhere in the world. Enable, disable, pause, or adjust settings from your phone, tablet, or computer — no need to be at your MT5 terminal.

  • Real-time EA status monitoring
  • Enable/disable trading with one tap
  • Per-symbol control (pause, resume, disable individual pairs)
  • Emergency stop button
  • Works on iPhone, Android, Windows, Mac
  • Installable as a mobile app (PWA)

🎯 Daily Profit Targets

Set a daily profit goal and Falcon AI automatically stops trading when it's reached. This protects your gains and prevents overtrading — one of the biggest account killers.

  • Configurable daily target percentage
  • Auto-stop when target is hit
  • Progress bar showing real-time P&L vs target
  • Admin can override targets remotely
  • Notification when target is reached

🛡️ Risk Management Suite

Multiple layers of protection ensure your capital is never at excessive risk. From position sizing to drawdown limits, every trade is managed with precision.

  • Maximum drawdown protection
  • Dynamic position sizing based on account equity
  • Per-trade stop loss and take profit
  • Emergency stop (kills all trading instantly)
  • Admin lock (admin can freeze any account remotely)
  • Local lock (EA self-locks on critical errors)

💬 Live Support & Messaging

Built-in chat system connects you directly with the admin team. No emails, no tickets lost in spam — instant communication right inside your dashboard.

  • Real-time chat with admin
  • File and image sharing
  • Support ticket system
  • Broadcast announcements from admin
  • Email notifications for new messages
  • Chat history preserved across sessions

🔐 Enterprise Security

Your account is protected by the same security standards used by financial institutions. Every communication is signed, every account is isolated.

  • HMAC-SHA256 signed requests (replay-proof)
  • Per-token account isolation
  • Brute-force protection on admin access
  • Anti-tamper detection
  • Audit trail for all admin actions
  • Private file storage (no public links)

📊 Account Monitoring

See your account performance at a glance — open positions, floating P&L, win rate, and more. All updated in real-time from your EA.

  • Live account balance and equity
  • Open position count and floating P&L
  • Today's wins, losses, and win rate
  • Session peak and drawdown tracking
  • Per-symbol performance stats

🌍 Multi-Language & Accessibility

Use Falcon AI in your preferred language. Full RTL support for Arabic and Farsi speakers.

  • English, Farsi (فارسی), Arabic (العربية), Turkish (Türkçe)
  • Right-to-left (RTL) layout support
  • Auto-detects browser language
  • Saves preference across sessions
