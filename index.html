Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What is Falcon AI?
Falcon AI is an advanced Expert Advisor (EA) for MetaTrader 5 that uses artificial intelligence to make trading decisions. It includes remote control, daily profit targets, risk management, and a full support system.
What broker can I use?
Falcon AI works with any MT5 broker. There are no broker restrictions. You use your own trading account.
Do I need to keep my computer running?
Yes, the EA runs inside MT5 on your computer or VPS. We recommend using a VPS for 24/7 operation. The remote dashboard works from any device.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time and your access will remain active until the end of the billing period.
Is my money safe?
Falcon AI trades on YOUR account with YOUR broker. We never have access to your funds. The EA includes built-in risk management, drawdown protection, and emergency stop features.
How do I get support?
Through the built-in live chat in your dashboard, support tickets, or email. Our team responds within hours.
